ABOUT ME


I was born in Peru and raised in Newark. I consider myself a musician and my favorite instrument is the Alto Sax and I have been playing it for about 10 years. I am also a self-taught pianist and i've made my own music using Digital Audio Workspace (DAW).

I was going to be a music major but it is very competative and in the long run I wouldn't like to exhaust myself doing something I like. Since I always had a passion for creating things and using any sort of technology, I see myself wanting to code and excel in it. Meanwhile I would make music as a hobby.
